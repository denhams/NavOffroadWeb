Overview

You can choose different map styles (3D terrain, satellite, hybrid) or even create your own! Maps can be downloaded ahead of time. NavOffroad is designed for environments where cellular service is unreliable or nonexistent.

Offroad trails

Download color-code offroad trails for an area. Get turn-by-turn navigation guidance along a trail.

Map feature information

Tap a feature on the map to get detailed information about it. You can share this with your friends!

Map styles

Create a © Mapbox account and use the Studio to create your style. Check out the Mapbox Gallery for examples.

Offline maps

Download map regions before heading out. Once downloaded, maps are fully usable without an internet connection.
